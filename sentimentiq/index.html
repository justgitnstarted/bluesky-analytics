The Six Dimensions

Every organizing campaign
traces back to a
breakdown here.

SentimentIQ measures all six simultaneously, weighted by their predictive relationship to union organizing interest — not general satisfaction or engagement, which flatten the dynamics that actually matter.

01
Management Effectiveness
Highest Predictor

Trust, fairness, and consistency of direct supervisors. The single strongest predictor of union interest across every industry studied. An employee who genuinely trusts their supervisor rarely concludes they need external representation.

Sample score: 2.1 / 5.0  ·  HIGH RISK
02
Communication Quality
High Predictor

Whether employees feel informed, included, and respected in how decisions are communicated. Poor communication is frequently the first dimension to deteriorate before organizing begins — and the easiest to address once identified.

Sample score: 2.7 / 5.0  ·  WATCH
03
Pay & Benefits
High Predictor

Perceived fairness of compensation relative to contribution and market — not absolute dollar amounts. Employees who feel their effort goes unrecognized become organizing candidates regardless of their actual pay level.

Sample score: 2.4 / 5.0  ·  WATCH
04
Safety & Work Conditions
Moderate Predictor

Physical environment, workload balance, scheduling equity, and whether employees believe the company genuinely prioritizes their wellbeing. Often a signal layer for deeper trust failures rather than a primary grievance on its own.

Sample score: 3.7 / 5.0  ·  OK
05
General Work Experience
Moderate Predictor

The overall emotional connection employees have with their job, team, and company. Belonging, pride, morale, meaning. Low scores indicate a workforce that has disengaged — a precondition for organizing interest, not a direct driver of it.

Sample score: 3.2 / 5.0  ·  OK
06
Union Sentiment Index
Proprietary Inference

Measured through validated proxy indicators woven throughout the assessment — not direct questions about union support, which create ULP exposure and produce unreliable data. The sixth dimension infers what it cannot ask. This is the methodological core that separates SentimentIQ from every general engagement tool on the market.

Engagement Process

Five steps.
Fourteen days.
Complete intelligence.

From roster intake to leadership debrief — designed to move at the pace your situation demands without sacrificing the rigor that makes the data trustworthy.

1
Secure Roster Intake

HR provides an employee roster — IDs and contact information only. We configure deployment around your shift structure, departments, and locations within 48 hours.

2
Anonymous Deployment

Each employee receives a unique, untraceable access code via SMS or email. No app download. No login. Participation tracking and response data are separated at the system level — not just the policy level.

3
14-Day Completion Window

Employees complete on their own time across all shifts and locations. You monitor real-time participation rates through a live dashboard — without ever seeing individual responses.

4
Analysis & Root Cause

Blue Sky processes responses through the SentimentIQ model and conducts structured interviews with management and employee cross-sections to identify the operational conditions driving each score.

5
Leadership Debrief & Roadmap

Full findings presentation — six-dimension scores, department and supervisor rankings, root cause analysis, and a phased gap-close roadmap with assigned owners and timelines. Executable the morning after the debrief.

What You Receive

Not a score. A plan.

Every SentimentIQ engagement delivers four concrete outputs. Not a report that sits in a folder. Intelligence that changes what leadership does the next morning.

Deliverable 01
Six-Dimension Sentiment Report

Scored results across all six dimensions — Management Effectiveness, Communication Quality, Pay & Benefits, Safety & Work Conditions, Work Experience, and Union Sentiment Index — with trend analysis and cross-location comparison where applicable.

Deliverable 02
Department & Supervisor Risk Ranking

Risk stratified by department, shift, and supervisor span of control. Not just which facility is vulnerable — but which supervisor's team is driving the aggregate score down. This is where the actionable intelligence lives.

Enhanced Tier
Deliverable 03
Root Cause Analysis

Operations-lens diagnosis of the specific conditions driving low scores — scheduling decisions, communication failures, management behaviors, compensation perception gaps. The source of the problem, not just the symptom score. Conducted through dual-phase structured interviews with management and employee cross-sections.

Enhanced Tier
Deliverable 04
Gap-Close Roadmap

A phased action plan bridging the current state to the desired state — a workplace where employees feel genuinely heard and valued by leadership. Specific actions, specific owners, specific timelines. Not a theory. A plan your team can execute starting the morning after the debrief.
